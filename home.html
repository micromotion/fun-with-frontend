What's this?

This is Quizinator, the website that deliver the best quiz in the world. Each quiz consist of 10 randomly selected questions from our question pool for you to answer. You can play the quiz right away without having to register, but if you do that you can't link a username to your stats.

What's the point?

Quizinator is fast pace, fun, and you'll learn quite a bit while playing. It's perfect when you're bored, got nothing else to do, or while you're waiting on the bus. You can even compare your highscore with your friends and claim the title of a quizmaster. Uh, just kidding, there's really no point of playing this game to be honest.

What else?

Please note that none of your personal information is stored by us. All information needed for keeping records of your highscore is handled by your web browser. This means that if you're for example using Chrome when answering the quiz, your score will only be remembered by that browser and not by any other browser you may have installed. Do not clear your web storage on your browser if you don't want to loose your highscore. You'll need to enable javascript to answer the quiz and your browser need to support web storage if you want to keep track of your highscore. Last but not least. Have fun!
